3D Printing Experience

Explore my journey in 3D printing, from founding Kóndoro to empowering communities through innovative digital fabrication.

Kóndoro

In 2014, I co-founded Kóndoro, a company dedicated to 3D printing and prototype manufacturing. Over more than seven years, I led projects encompassing modeling, manufacturing 3D printers, and knowledge management in digital fabrication, including talks, consulting, training, and demonstrations.

Community Engagement

Between 2017 and 2018, I served as a project manager at the non-profit organization "Materialización 3D" (M3D), aiming to train individuals with limited resources and/or functional diversities in emerging technologies, promoting inclusion and access to digital fabrication.

Workshops and Conferences

I have conducted workshops and lectures on 3D printing at various educational institutions and events, sharing knowledge about materials, technologies, and applications in this field. These activities have contributed to the dissemination and adoption of digital fabrication across different communities.

Publications

I have created academic and literary content related to 3D printing, including presentations and articles exploring technical parameters, materials, and technologies in this domain. Some of my presentations are available on SlideShare.
